Training progammes

Here at Juniorbreakers we offer a range of sports and activities to help support our community. Starting at Saturday drills and skills to an Academy course after school program lasting a first school term and finally to Camp which will focus on a 2 week intensive routine to help sharpen the skills of aspiring youths.

Membership

At Junior Breakers, our Membership subscription offers special discounts to programmes run by Our Organization from Saturday Drills and Skill session discounts to Academy discounts and Basketball Camp too. Another feature of the Membership is also free access to special an on site Gym on Sundays where people Training could also help train their muscles to keep an edge in basketball games.
